Diving is one of the most complect sports there is, so that's why it requires a lot of focus and practice to be able to dive. When diving off a board, you are required to have your hand, on the other hand, clasping together tightly and having your bottom palm flat. You also need to have your arms stretched out and above your head. This is important to use this technique depending on the type of dive.

Your body will always enter the water straight with any dive you are performing. By entering the water straight you are preventing yourself from any serious injuries. When diving it is highly important to land properly. If you land in the right position for a dive where you're going in head first it's called a rip entry. A rip entry is where the diver enters the water with no splash and makes a ripping sound from their hands in the water.
