Conclusion

This was a great way to help me seek further in becoming a more confident diver and understanding the history behind diving. One thing I would have liked to do differently was to dig deeper into researching more on diving. I loved seeing my project come together and get other people’s opinions on ideas or suggestions to make my project better. A challenge that I had to work around was researching certain questions as diving was a hard topic, but I managed to find answers and research to help me answer my questions. Also coming up with questions was a challenge but I kept working at it and if ended up with a good wide range of questions for diving. I developed a different range of skills throughout my project, some of them were taking other people’s ideas on board and giving them a go, working with a website page I was unfamiliar with and adapting to that and building on my research skills by not only looking online but also in books or videos. By developing these skills, it has successfully helped me pursue my project and to show my passion for diving.
